	Thought Process	
Here is the breakdown of which approach is best for your 24 MB Excel file, based on speed, ease of use, and safety.
The Short Answer
Keep it in Local Storage (GitHub Repo).
However, do not keep it as an .xlsx file. Convert it to .parquet or .csv first.
 Why: Reading a 24 MB .xlsx file is slow because Streamlit has to unzip and parse the XML structure every time the app runs or re-runs.
 The Fix: A 24 MB Excel file converted to Parquet will likely be only 3-5 MB and will load 10x to 50x faster.
